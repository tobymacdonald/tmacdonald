After working with Marc on his EP, we decided to form a band. The initial members were me, Marc and my friend Kirsty. Together we worked on our debut EP "Subliminal", which released later in the same year. I think I released so much music in 2018 as it was my final year before university, and I think I knew how little time I would have to work on music once my undergraduate studies began. 

 The opening track for this EP was well recieved by critics, with The Herald ranking it as the fourth best song to come from a Scottish artist in 2018.
